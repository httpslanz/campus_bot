Intelligent Chatbot for Campus Services of Lipa City Colleges Using Text Mining

User Manual

This user manual will guide you on how to use the Intelligent Chatbot for Campus Services of Lipa City Colleges using Text Mining.

1 Introduction

The Intelligent Chatbot for Campus Services of Lipa City Colleges is a web-based system designed to assist students, applicants, and other users by providing instant and accurate answers to frequently asked questions about admissions, enrollment, academic programs, scholarships, and other campus services.

2 Accessing the System
  1. 1 Open a web browser (Google Chrome, Edge, etc.)
  2. 2
    Visit the system link:
    https://chatbot-lcc.up.railway.app/
  3. 3 The chatbot interface will appear on the screen.
3 How to Use the Chatbot
1
Click the chat input field.
2
Type your question.
3
Press Enter or click the send button.
4
Wait for the chatbot to respond.
4 What Can the Chatbot Assist You With?
Academic programs offered by the college
Admission requirements for incoming students
Application process for new students
Required documents for admission
Enrollment procedures for freshmen
Admission schedules and deadlines
Scholarship programs offered by the school
Where and how to apply for scholarships
Admission office contact information
5 Example Questions
What academic programs are offered?
What are the admission requirements?
How can I apply for admission?
What documents are needed for freshmen?
How do I enroll as a new student?
What scholarship programs are available?
Where can I apply for scholarships?
6 Tips for Better Results
Use clear and simple questions.
Avoid long or unrelated sentences.
Focus on one question at a time.
Use keywords such as: admission, enrollment, scholarship, requirements, application, deadlines, etc.
7 System Limitations
The chatbot only answers questions related to campus services.
It may not respond correctly to out-of-scope questions.
Responses depend on the data and training of the system.
8 Troubleshooting
Issue Solution
No response from chatbot
 Refresh the page or try again later.
Incorrect answer
 Try rephrasing your question.
Website not loading
 Check your internet connection.
9 Conclusion

The Intelligent Chatbot enhances access to campus information by providing fast, automated, and user-friendly assistance. It reduces the need for manual inquiries and improves the overall experience of students and applicants.
